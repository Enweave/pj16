Mini platformer with shapeshifting

Controls

  • A,D - move
  • J, LMB - use element
  • Space - jump
  • 1,2,3 - switch element
  • W,S - aim up/down
  • Escape, P - menu

Story

You fight back adventurers that try to do quests in your forest. Prove them you're not a quest objective!
